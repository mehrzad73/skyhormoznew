MANILA TO DUBAI PACKAGE

✥   Bring your family to witness the Essential Beauty of Dubai, you may sponsor your spouse, Kid/s, Family and Relatives (up to 2ND Degree).


Inclusions

  • Original UAE visa (1 Month / 3 Months)
  • Invitation letter
  • One way flight ticket from Manila to Dubai
  • Return ticket assistance
  • Affidavit of support assistance
  • Courier Fee for all the Documents

What do I need to apply ?

Passenger/Visitor

  • Scanned Passport copy (valid minimum for 6 months)
  • Passport Size photographs with white background
  • Scanned PSA Birth certificate copy

Sponsors / Guarantor

  • Passport copy and visa page copy (both valid for minimum 3 months)
  • Salary Certificate or Labour Contract
  • OWWA copy or OEC copy
  • Proof of relationship documents
          Ex. Marriage Contract
            PSA Birth Certificate, and other

Note- Package rate is on subject to availability.

  • Security Deposit will apply depends on Nationality
  • In case of all kind of over stays or absconding of applicant, all the fines will be paid by Applicant / Sponsor of application.
